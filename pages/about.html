Ethan Miller LLP is a trial and litigation boutique built for high-impact cases.

We have decades of experience litigating at the highest levels, and we take on cases where failure is not an option.  We zealously defend our clients in high stakes government investigations and enforcement actions.  And we pursue financial recovery for individuals and companies who have been defrauded by bad actors on the blockchain or by other means.

We are founded and led by two former federal prosecutors who brought pioneering fraud cases that resulted in the recovery of over $5 billion in ill-gotten gains. After government service, we were partners in two of the country's most prestigious law firms, where we represented individuals and companies in some of the highest-profile, highest-stakes government investigations and litigations of our time.  

We created this boutique to deliver top-tier firepower to clients who need fearless, strategic advocacy with an agility that traditional law firms cannot match.  And we seek to deliver exceptional results in cases where nothing less will do.
