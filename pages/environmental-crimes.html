A Science-Driven Defense Approach

Environmental crimes defense demands technical sophistication beyond traditional legal advocacy. Our methodology incorporates:

  • Scientific Expert Collaboration: We partner with leading environmental scientists, engineers, and technical specialists to challenge government sampling, testing protocols, and conclusions
  • Regulatory Navigation: We leverage deep understanding of complex environmental permitting regimes to identify compliance defenses and regulatory ambiguities
  • Parallel Proceedings Management: We coordinate defense strategy across simultaneous criminal, civil, and administrative proceedings to optimize outcomes
  • Crisis Response: We provide immediate counsel during environmental incidents, managing evidence preservation, government communications, and public relations concerns
  • Strategic Engagement: When appropriate, we pursue consent decrees, deferred prosecution agreements, and compliance programs that address government concerns while protecting client interests
