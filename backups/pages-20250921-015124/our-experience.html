Our Experience

Unmatched Depth in High-Stakes Litigation

Founding partners Ethan Miller and Arlo Devlin-Brown have between them more than five decades of elite experience representing private clients and the Department of Justice in some of the most consequential financial fraud cases of our time.

Seeking Recovery For Fraud Victims As Federal Prosecutors 

We served for a combined 20 years as federal prosecutors and supervisors in the Office of the U.S. Attorney for the Southern District of New York—the nation’s premier prosecutor’s office. There, we led landmark prosecutions that reshaped entire industries and ultimately recovered over $5 billion in ill-gotten gains, much of which was distributed to fraud victims. 

Elite Private Practice In The Nation’s Top Law Firms

For an additional 25 years between us, we served as partners in the white-collar defense and litigation practices of two leading AmLaw 50 firms, where we represented Fortune 100 companies and C-suite executives in bet-the-company investigations and litigations across the globe.

Courtroom Credibility

We have collectively tried more than 25 federal cases to verdict and argued a similar number of appeals. This extensive trial experience informs every aspect of our practice—ensuring confidence in litigation, creating leverage in negotiations, and providing strategic advantages at every stage of representation.
